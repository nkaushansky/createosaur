Where AI fits

  
In the real product, this button sends the genome + this exact render (not a text
  prompt) to an image-to-image model. The result is a painterly portrait that keeps your creature's
  proportions, parts and markings — consistent every time, because the deterministic render anchors it.

  
That makes AI the slow, gorgeous reward at the end of the loop — and a natural paid
  moment — instead of the loop itself.
